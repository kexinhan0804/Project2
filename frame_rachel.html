Services Details

Hang-Gliding Adventures
  1. Individual Sessions - guided by experienced instructors, tailored to your skill level and preferences.
  2. Group Sessions - share the thrill of flight and build camaraderie while receiving expert guidance.

Training & Courses

 Classes - Our courses are led by experienced instructors who provide expert guidance. Upon completion, participants have the opportunity to obtain hang-gliding certification.
  • Research regulations before coming to your certification courses: Paragliding Regulations PDF
  • Come prepared! To receive your full certification, you must be fully prepared with your own equipment. Refer to the following website to know what you must have: Paragliding Equipment

Equipment Cleaning & Repair
  • Keep your equipment in top shape with our reliable Cleaning & Repair services.
  • Our technicians offer routine maintenance and handle all repair needs, ensuring your gear stays reliable.

Hang-Gliding Socials
  • Join our tight-knit hang-gliding community at our weekly socials.
  • Every Wednesday at 6:00 PM at the clubhouse.
  • $5 entry fee covers food and drinks.
  • Age requirements: 21 or older.
