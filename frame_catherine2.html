Meet the Team!
We are a small family business proud of everything we have to offer. Let's introduce you to some of our employees and members of the family. Our employees deserve so much credit for all of the work that they do.


  • Harry and Harriet
    • Our fearless leaders who started it all. While they are retired now, they still play a big part in our company. They still help out around the shop and love interacting with customers.

  • Hadley
    • Harry and Harriet's oldest daughter who has taken over the reins. Hadley loves what she does and is so proud of the work she does for Harry and Harriet's hang-gliding Haven. Hadley loves spending time with her 2 german shepherds and her husband Ray. They both spend a lot of time hang-gliding together and their favorite place is Yellowstone.

  • Hudson
    • Harry and Harriet's oldest son who is an adventurer at heart. Hudson is our top hang-gliding specialist who trains employees as well as groups. He is our top guide and takes hang-gliding trips with our customers. His favorite hang-gliding spot is anywhere with water and he loves to go up and watch the sunsets. Hudson loves to hanglide with his wife Molly who is our shop manager.

  • Molly
    • Hudson's wife and beloved hanglider. She loves to hanglide in the mountains and is down for all of the trips. She runs our shop in the day to day and loves picking out new gear for all of our customers.

  • Hayden, Harrison, and Henry
    • Hudson and Molly's triplet boys. The boys have grown up hang-gliding and love to share the love of the sport with their friends. When they aren't in the air or in school they are working as shop associates helping out their mom.

  • Rob
    • Rob is one of our oldest employees and knows all areas of the shop. He is one of our instructors and loves to get to know everyone. His favorite place to hanglide is farms
